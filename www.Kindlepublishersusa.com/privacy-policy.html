Personal Data You Subtly Give Us

By using our website or services, you freely provide us your Personal Information.

We will gather all the information required to complete the transaction when you place an order for goods or services on the website, including your name, credit card number, billing information, and shipping information.

In the event that you consent to receive promotional emails about Our services, we will gather your email address when you sign up for one of our newsletters. By following the unsubscribe instructions in the newsletter you get or by getting in touch with us, you can discontinue the newsletter service at any time.

We will gather your name, email address, and the comments, questions, or feedback you provide when utilizing the forms on the website or in the services to get in touch with us and respond to your inquiries, worries, or requests.

Posting posts on the Website's message boards or through the Services will result in the information you post being saved on our systems and made visible to other users.

When you email us with any questions or concerns, we will gather and keep your email address, any information you include in the email, and any attachments for as long as is required. We use your information to respond to your email and handle any queries or worries you might have.

Please be aware that any content you submit to other members for their books or utilize for your own books may also contain personal information. You should only use and contribute Content that you are comfortable sharing under these conditions and for which you have obtained (where applicable) all necessary and valid consents to share and disclose. Please be aware that such Content may be made available to others (including through the use of the Book preview functionality) as content of your Books or the Books of other Members to which you contributed Content.

Personal Data Accessed Through The Service Platform

Our does not directly interact with the people whose personal data it processes; rather, it gathers information at the request of its clients. Please get in touch with the client you deal with directly if you are a customer of one of our clients and would prefer not to be approached by any clients that utilize our service. We might give personal data to businesses that assist us in delivering our service. The service agreements we have with our clients cover transfers to later third parties.

Personal Data Against Using Tracking Technology

Certain data, such as your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, operating system, mouse clicks, mouse movements, and scrolling behavior, may also be passively collected as you use our website or app. Additionally, we collect information about the date and time of your visit as well as the solutions and information you searched for and viewed using Cookies and navigational data like Uniform Resource Locators (URL). This kind of data is gathered in order to assess how our solutions and services are used and to improve their value to you.

Personal Data Obtained From Other Sources

We might get Personal Information about you from other sources, such as a fax, phone, or mobile device, or from businesses that use a co-branded or private-labeled website to deliver our services. For some of our services, this also includes location-based services that are relevant to particular features of the services. It's possible that some parts of the Services won't function properly if you decide to disable location-based services. To enhance the goods and services we offer, we might integrate this data to the data we have already gathered from you through our website or other services.

Information Use

Generally speaking, we use the Personal Information you give us to either better serve you or to reply to your requests. We may also use your Personal Information to, provide you with the Our newsletters, surveys, or promotional emails regarding changes and upgrades to our products and services and for other marketing purposes of Our or our partners, should you request and consent to receive such communications from us. We care about the security of your information. We can't guarantee the complete security of your personal information, even though we make every effort to safeguard it using commercially reasonable methods.
